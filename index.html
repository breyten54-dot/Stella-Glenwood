Our Story

Nestled in the heart of Glenwood, Stella is a place where bold flavors meet relaxed elegance. Our kitchen celebrates seasonal ingredients, local producers, and the joy of sharing a great meal with good company.

From intimate dinners to lively celebrations, every plate is crafted with care and every guest is treated like family.
